Gear

Your weapon is your moveset. Every piece has a stat line anyone can use — and an (Expertise) verb that only opens up once you're trained in it.

How to read a weapon. Atk is added to your attack rolls, Def to your defense rolls — both on top of the ability modifier matching the weapon's class (Heavy → Strength, Medium → Strength or Dexterity, Light → Dexterity, Ranged → Awareness). Only your primary weapon contributes its stats; shields and armor add or subtract on top.
