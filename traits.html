Traits

Your weapon is your moveset — your Traits are your identity. They shape how you relate to everything around the swordplay: tempo, Poise, Grit, and your allies.

Trait Slots. You may have two Traits equipped at a time at level one, and you can freely swap between known Traits during a rest. Slots open up as you advance — commit for the road ahead, not forever.

Aggression & Guard-Breaking

Guardbreaker
Hits you land that deal at least 3 Grit raise the target's Poise by an additional point, and forced movement builds Poise at +1 per 5 ft instead of per 10 ft.
Bloodletter
You can use the Wound maneuver for two points instead of three.
Overwhelm
Once per scene, spend a Hope: your next attack builds double Poise and cannot be Perfect Parried.

Defense & Counter

Duelist's Eye
Your defense die counts as one higher or lower when checking for a Perfect Parry.
Ironclad
+1 Armor Slot innately, and you recover an Armor Slot whenever you take a rest.
Bulwark
While wielding a shield, allies within reach of you gain +1 Def.

Tempo & Fortune

Battle Trance
Once per scene, when an odd roll would flip initiative, you keep it instead.
Momentum
The first attack you make each time initiative shifts to your team is made at advantage.

Command & Support

Field Medic
You can treat a Wound mid-combat as an action (DC varying by severity), and your Rally maneuver also clears one Wound from an ally within reach.
Warleader
Once per scene, you may arm a Setup on every ally within reach.
Inspiring Presence
Your Rally maneuver affects one additional ally.
Coordinated Strike
When you attack a target that already has a Setup on it, you gain +1 to that attack.

Survivor & Grit

Hardy
+2 to your maximum Grit.
Second Wind
Your Recomposure recovery roll succeeds on a 13+ instead of a 16.
Adrenaline
While at half Grit or below, you gain +1 to attack rolls.
Undying
Once per rest, when you would make a Death Move, you instead stay up at 1 Grit.

Building a character

Traits stack into recognizable archetypes — the same weapons play completely differently depending on what you slot:

  • The Wall — Ironclad + Bulwark: a shield-bearer who shelters the line and refuses to fall.
  • The Fencer — Duelist's Eye + Battle Trance: a counter-fighter who lives on Perfect Parries and owns the tempo.
  • The Bloodhound — Guardbreaker + Bloodletter: a berserker who shatters guards and leaves lasting harm.
  • The Captain — Warleader + Inspiring Presence: weak alone, devastating with a party at their back.
